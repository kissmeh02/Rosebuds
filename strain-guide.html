Strain Guide

Use this guide to quickly understand common strain categories and terpene profiles.

Indica

Often associated with relaxing, body-forward effects and evening use.

Sativa

Often associated with uplifting, energetic effects and daytime use.

Hybrid

Can combine traits from indica and sativa, often offering balanced effects.

Terpenes Matter

  • Limonene: citrus-forward, often uplifting.
  • Myrcene: earthy profile, often relaxing.
  • Caryophyllene: spicy/pepper profile, often grounding.
  • Linalool: floral profile, often calming.
  • Pinene: pine profile, often clear-headed.

How To Choose

  • Start with desired effect: focus, calm, social, or sleep support.
  • Match product type to your comfort level and onset preference.
  • Choose lower THC and smaller doses if you're new.
