Cannabis 101

Welcome to the basics. This guide is educational only and intended for Ontario adults 19+.

Product Types

  • Flower: Dried cannabis buds, commonly smoked or vaporized.
  • Pre-rolls: Ready-to-use joints with convenient dosing.
  • Vapes: Portable, discreet inhalation with faster onset.
  • Edibles: Slower onset, longer duration; start low and be patient.
  • Oils/Tinctures: Flexible dose control, often used sublingually.

THC vs CBD

  • THC is primarily responsible for intoxicating effects.
  • CBD is non-intoxicating and often chosen for wellness-focused use.
  • Balanced products may offer milder, more manageable effects for new users.

Dosing Basics

  • Start low, go slow, especially with edibles.
  • For edibles, wait 2 hours before increasing your dose.
  • Do not mix with alcohol until you know your tolerance.

Important Safety Notes

  • Never drive or operate machinery while impaired.
  • Keep products locked away from children and pets.
  • Use only as permitted by Ontario laws and regulations.
