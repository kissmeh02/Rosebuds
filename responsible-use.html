Responsible Use

Safe consumption starts with informed choices and clear limits.

Core Guidelines

  • Only for adults 19+ in Ontario.
  • Start low and increase slowly over time.
  • Avoid mixing cannabis with alcohol.
  • Keep products secured and out of reach of children/pets.

Impairment Safety

  • Do not drive, cycle, or operate machinery while impaired.
  • Plan transportation before consuming.
  • Impairment can last longer than expected, especially with edibles.

If You Feel Uncomfortable

  • Move to a calm environment and hydrate.
  • Avoid additional THC and rest.
  • If symptoms feel severe, seek medical support immediately.
